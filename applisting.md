## UPDATED FAQ CONTENT

**Q: How is Quoffer different from Offerly?**
A: Quoffer offers the same instant auto-accept automation as Offerly, but with ZERO commission fees. Plus we include professional B2B features like price hiding, custom quote forms, and full Shopify Draft Order support that Offerly doesn't offer. You get automation + advanced features for a simple flat monthly fee.

**Q: How does auto-accept work?**
A: You set price thresholds (e.g., "auto-accept offers ≥95% of product price"). When customers submit offers meeting your threshold, Quoffer automatically creates a Shopify Draft Order and sends them a checkout link via email—no manual work required. Offers below your threshold can be auto-rejected with counter-offers or sent to manual review.

**Q: Can I turn off automation and use it manually?**
A: Yes! Auto-accept is completely optional. You can use Quoffer as a traditional RFQ app where you review every quote manually. Or use automation for some products and manual review for others. It's flexible.

**Q: What happens to quotes between my thresholds?**
A: Quotes in your "manual review zone" (e.g., between 70-95%) go to your dashboard inbox. You get a notification and can review them using one-click quick actions: accept as-is, accept with discount, send counter-offer, or decline.

**Q: Do I pay commission on auto-accepted quotes?**
A: No! Unlike Offerly's 1% commission, Quoffer charges zero commission on any quotes—automated or manual. You only pay the flat monthly plan fee based on how many won quotes you process per month.

**Q: Will automation break my theme or slow my site?**
A: No. Quoffer uses Shopify's native app embed system (not theme code injection). It's 100% theme-safe, has zero impact on page speed, and can be enabled/disabled with one click in your theme editor.

**Q: How fast is "instant" auto-accept?**
A: When a customer submits a qualifying offer, the Draft Order is created and checkout email is sent within 10 seconds. Customers get immediate confirmation and can complete their purchase right away.

**Q: Can I customize the automated emails?**
A: Yes. All email templates (auto-accepted, counter-offer, manual review, etc.) can be customized with your branding, colors, logo, and custom messages. You control what customers see.
